Welcome to the Colemine label and Plaid Room record shop. We are an existing label and new record store built from the hard work of two brothers, with a simple goal to enrich the musical landscape we live in. We work tirelessly to acquire vinyl of all types, both new and used. So come in and check us out seven days a week.
